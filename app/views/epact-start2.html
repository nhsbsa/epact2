Register to use ePACT2

To apply to use ePACT2, you'll need to complete all the sections of this form.

Before you start, make sure you've read:

  • the registration guidance on the NHS Business Service Authority (NHSBSA) website
  • the ePACT2 user agreement (EUA)

NHSBSA provide training on how to use ePACT2. you can find out more:

  • on the NHSBSA website
  • by contacting the training team at nhsbsa.epact2training@nhs.net

Once your account is active, your user ID will be deactivated if you do not access the system for 6 months.
